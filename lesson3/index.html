Site Purpose

The purpose of the Silverton Chamber of Commerce is to help people to learn about the town of Silverton, Oregon. They will be able to learn about the town’s history, local businesses, attractions, as well as the geography and weather. It will be beneficial for town visitors, perspective people moving to the area, or even locals that want a little more information.
