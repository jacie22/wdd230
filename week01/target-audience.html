Personas

Persona 1: Gayle fits in the stereotypical PNW box. She is single and in her 30s. She loves to hike, travel, and go thrift shopping. She is all about being eco friendly and ops to buy organic products when available. She has a mid-size dog who she treats like her child and takes almost everywhere with her. Hiking on the weekends is always expected, and she’s trying to grow her social media following by posting hiking pictures all the time. She works for an organic farming company as their social media manager but doesn’t make a lot of money. She ties to wear/use high quality clothing/gear but it’s typically bought second hand. Gayle is looking for cute towns to visit where she can take her dog, go hiking, and enjoy an afternoon of shopping and eating.

Persona 2: Faustus is in his mid 20’s and just got a job working for a start up tech company in nearby Salem. He plans to spend a lot of time working, but when he’s off, he wants to be able to relax in a smaller hometown. He is newly married, and his wife is a nurse. They want to start having children soon but are concerned about the school and town where they should locate to. Faustus loves to watch the college football games and go hunting in his spare time. Faustus is looking to settle down and needs to be able to research a town before making the decision to move.

Scenarios

  1. Does Silverton have any outdoor activities for young families?

    2. Faustus is trying to determine if Silverton will be a good place to settle down for his young family and is trying to research about how family friendly the town is. Visiting families can find a whole webpage dedicated to the local businesses and attractions with special categories that are family friendly.

  2. Where is the best place to have brunch after a hike in Silverton?

    3. Gayle found a hike to take her dog on, but she needs to make sure that she can go out to eat with her pup later that morning. The Chamber of Commerce website details the popular food establishments and links to the company website and menu to make choosing a brunch spot easy. There is even information stating if the establishment is pet friendly.

  3. Where can I learn more about the historic locations and history of the town of Silverton?

    4. A local high school student is trying to do a town research paper and needs to find some of the local historical locations as well as the story behind them. The Silverton Chamber of commerce website will have a page dedicated to the town’s history with photos and descriptions. Eventually a historical tour map will be available for visitors to download.

  4. How can I contribute to the town and tourism?

    5. A longtime resident and business owner in the town of Silverton wants to help bring more business to the small town. The website has a page dedicated to joining the Chamber of Commerce and helping the website improve and thus bringing more information to the public to bring in more visitors.

  5. What type of clothing should I pack based on the weather and time of year?

    6. Potential travelers to the area want to make sure they are properly dresses and equipped to visit the town of Silverton during any time of the year. The home page will show the daily weather with a section describing the local climate through the year.
